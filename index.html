During this period he was unable to stream on his channel due to lack of internet and hardware competent enough to handle such taxing tasks. He subsequently managed to get a secondhand PC after multiple teaching posts in a local institute. GamerFleet's actual rise to Success came following a Stream Raid on one of his streams by comedian Samay Raina and Chessmaster Vidit Gujrathi which boosted his audience drastically. Following this he began playing FallGuys with Samay Raina,Karan singh Magic and Suhani Shah. This Boosted GamerFleet's following by a thousand-fold. He has since then been grinding with Minecraft.
